It's the end of the day and you have six notes to finish. Instead of starting from a blank template, tell Lumen the key details and get a draft you can review and sign.

## What to Type

Describe the visit the way you'd tell a colleague about it:

- *"Write a SOAP note for a 55yo male with uncontrolled HTN, BP 168/96 today, added amlodipine 5mg, follow up 2 weeks"*
- *"Discharge summary: 68yo CHF exacerbation, IV diuresis x3 days, improved, discharge on lasix 40mg"*
- *"Referral letter to cardiology for a 42yo with new murmur and exertional dyspnea"*

## Note Types Lumen Can Draft

| Just Ask For... | What You Get |
|-----------------|-------------|
| *"SOAP note"* | Subjective, Objective, Assessment, Plan |
| *"H&P"* | Full history and physical |
| *"Discharge summary"* | Admission diagnosis, course, meds, follow-up |
| *"Referral letter"* | Professional referral with clinical context |
| *"Progress note"* | Inpatient daily progress |

## Tips

- **More detail = better draft.** Include vitals, meds, labs, and your plan
- **You can edit after.** The note is a starting point — adjust wording, add details, remove anything that doesn't fit
- **Copy or download** the final note from the side panel

{{< callout type="important" >}}
**Always review before signing.** Lumen drafts the note; you verify the accuracy and sign off per your institution's policy.
