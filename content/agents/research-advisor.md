You have a clinical question that requires a real evidence review — not just a quick answer, but what the studies show, what the FDA says, and whether any trials are running. The Research Advisor handles the whole thing.

## Give It Your Question

> *"What is the current evidence for SGLT2 inhibitors in heart failure with preserved ejection fraction?"*
