Protocols & Decision Support

When you need to work through a clinical problem systematically — building a differential, choosing an antibiotic, following a protocol — Lumen does the structured work so you can focus on the patient.

Available Tools

☑
Clinical Checklists
Evidence-based protocol checklists for sepsis bundles, DKA management, central line insertion, surgical safety, and more. Interactive — track completion by checking items.
"Give me a sepsis 3-hour bundle checklist"
Protocols
🏥
Triage Scoring
Emergency triage systems — ESI (Emergency Severity Index), MTS (Manchester Triage), CTAS, and START. Scores patient acuity from clinical presentation.
"Triage a 55-year-old with chest pain, diaphoresis, and BP 90/60"
Emergency
🧠
Differential Diagnosis
Generates a ranked differential diagnosis based on symptoms, demographics, vitals, and history. Searches PubMed and maps to ICD-10. Includes distinguishing features and recommended workup.
"Differential for a 30-year-old with acute onset pleuritic chest pain and dyspnea"
Diagnosis
🦠
Antimicrobial Selector
Selects appropriate antibiotic therapy based on IDSA guidelines. Considers infection type, severity, allergies, and pregnancy status. Returns first/second-line regimens with dosing and duration.
"Best antibiotic for community-acquired pneumonia, PCN allergy, outpatient"
Treatment
💥
Allergy Cross-Reactivity
Identifies cross-reactive drugs and safe alternatives for patients with known drug allergies. Maps allergy patterns and suggests de-sensitization options.
"What can I use instead of cephalexin if the patient has a penicillin allergy?"
Safety
📖
Clinical Guidelines
Searches USPSTF and PubMed for practice guidelines (2020+). Returns recommendations with letter grades and evidence summaries.
"Current guidelines for managing newly diagnosed hypertension"
Guidelines
👍
Clinical Recommendations
Generates prioritized, evidence-graded recommendations (Grade A through D). Context-specific with supporting rationale.
"Recommendations for secondary stroke prevention in a patient with PFO"
Evidence
💳
NPI Lookup
Searches the NPI provider registry. Resolves provider name, specialty, taxonomy, and practice location. Useful for referrals and verification.
"Look up cardiologists in Austin, TX"
Directory
