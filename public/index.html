How It Works

Three steps. That’s it.

1

Type your question

Use plain English. Ask the same way you’d text a colleague: “Can I give metoprolol if the patient’s on verapamil?”

2

Lumen finds the answer

Behind the scenes, Lumen searches clinical databases, checks guidelines, and runs the right tools automatically.

3

You get a clear, sourced answer

Read the response, check the sources if you want, and tap any reports or checklists. That’s it — back to your patient.
