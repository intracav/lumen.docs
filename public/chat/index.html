The Chat

Lumen works like a text conversation. You type a question, Lumen gives you a clear answer with evidence. These guides explain everything you'll see on screen.

If you’ve used iMessage or WhatsApp, you already know how to use Lumen. Your messages go on the right. Lumen’s answers appear on the left. The guides below walk you through the details — like how to read the sources, what those cards below the answer are, and how to upload a document for Lumen to read.
