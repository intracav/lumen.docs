Research Advisor

You have a clinical question that requires a real evidence review — not just a quick answer, but what the studies show, what the FDA says, and whether any trials are running. The Research Advisor handles the whole thing.

Give It Your Question

“What is the current evidence for SGLT2 inhibitors in heart failure with preserved ejection fraction?”

The 7-Step Review

1
Extract Research Terms
Identifies the key concepts and builds optimized search queries.
📄
Search Strategy
Terms: SGLT2 inhibitors, HFpEF, dapagliflozin, empagliflozin, heart failure preserved ejection fraction
Filters: RCTs, systematic reviews, meta-analyses, 2020–2026
2
Knowledge Base Search
Searches Lumen's indexed clinical content for baseline context.
📚
Clinical Context4 sources
ACC/AHA HF Guidelines · ESC HFpEF Position Paper · SGLT2i Class Review · HFpEF Diagnostic Criteria
3
PubMed LiteraturePubMed
Searches PubMed for peer-reviewed studies, fetches abstracts, ranks by relevance.
📚
PubMed Results12 articles
Solomon SD et al. DELIVER (2022)Dapagliflozin in HFpEF. NEJM. HR 0.82 (0.73–0.92) for worsening HF or CV death.
Anker SD et al. EMPEROR-Preserved (2021)Empagliflozin in HFpEF. NEJM. HR 0.79 (0.69–0.90) for CV death or HF hospitalization.
Vaduganathan M et al. (2022)SGLT2i across the HF spectrum: meta-analysis. Lancet.
4
FDA Drug DataOpenFDA
Checks FDA prescribing information, approved indications, and safety data.
💊
FDA Label Data
Dapagliflozin (Farxiga)✓ FDA-approved for HF (all EFs)
Empagliflozin (Jardiance)✓ FDA-approved for HF (all EFs)
Key warnings:DKA risk, genital infections, volume depletion
5
Clinical TrialsClinicalTrials.gov
Searches for active and recently completed trials relevant to the question.
🧪
Active Trials3 trials
NCT05396573SGLT2i + GLP-1 RA combination in HFpEF (Phase 3, recruiting)
NCT06012839Dapagliflozin in HFpEF with obesity (Phase 4, active)
6
Drug NormalizationRxNorm
Resolves drug names to canonical identifiers for accuracy.
💊
Drug Profile
Class:SGLT2 inhibitor (sodium-glucose co-transporter 2)
Mechanism:Blocks glucose reabsorption in proximal tubule → glycosuria, osmotic diuresis, cardioprotection
✓
Evidence Synthesis
Combines all evidence into a structured review with conclusions and identified gaps.
📋
Literature ReviewFinal Report
Conclusion: Strong evidence (2 large RCTs, meta-analyses) supports SGLT2i use in HFpEF. Both dapagliflozin and empagliflozin FDA-approved. NNT ~21 for HF hospitalization prevention. Active trials exploring combination therapy and subpopulations.
💡 Tip
Attach a paper for context
Upload a research paper in the Sources panel. The agent will use it as context — finding related studies, newer data, or contradicting evidence.
